How it works
01

Parse your query

Natural-language input processed by a local Qwen LLM. No data sent to any cloud. Extracts origin, destination, budget, and mode preferences.

02

Expand nearby airports

Origin expanded to every airport within 400 km reachable by ground transport. Nantes becomes NTE, CDG, ORY, BVA, RNS, BOD.

03

Search the multimodal graph

Yen's k-shortest-paths runs across a graph where every flight, train, and bus is an independent edge. Finds combinations no OTA assembles.

04

Per-leg platform selection

Each leg is priced across 6 platforms. The cheapest platform wins that leg. Three legs, three booking links, one total price that beats everything.

What makes this different

Unbundled routing

Google finds the cheapest within one booking ecosystem. We find the cheapest by assembling the best price for each leg from wherever it's cheapest — Kiwi for budget EU, MakeMyTrip for South Asia, Omio for trains.

Multi-stop self-transfer

Google caps at 1 self-transfer. We search 2, 3, 4-stop combinations freely. NTE → KTW → DXB → DEL uses three carriers with no agreements between them. That route doesn't exist on any single platform.

Secondary hub routing

Katowice (KTW), Belgrade (BEG), Tbilisi (TBS), Sharjah (SHJ) — budget carrier hubs with low airport costs that make routes like NTE → KTW (€22 Ryanair) possible and visible.

Explore / Anywhere mode

Set a budget, we show every destination reachable from your city. Single-source Dijkstra across the full graph in one pass. "Where can I go this weekend for €150?" — answered instantly.

Travel Finder is an independent student project at École Centrale de Nantes, developed as part of the Pépite SNEE student-entrepreneur programme.

The project uses the SNCF / Navitia API to provide real European rail schedule data as part of its multimodal route engine — combining it with flight data from Amadeus and bus data from FlixBus to build a genuinely comprehensive travel search.

All processing runs locally. No user data is stored or sent to third parties beyond the booking platforms shown in results.
